The Five Vows

§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§
Vow 1
Purpose:
Alleviate Suffering

Witness Woe,
Plant Wisdom's Seed:
Boundless Honeylight

§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§
Vow 2
Method:
Graceful Power

Don't contend,
Nourish all:
be Water.

§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§
Vow 3
Conduct:
Don't be an Asshole

Think, speak, act:
Kindness,
never ill.

§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§
Vow 4
Stance:
Unwavering Integrity

Stay back,
in front;
stay outside,
within.

§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§-§
Vow 5
Growth:
Profound Evolution

From flux,
learn;
refine the lens.
